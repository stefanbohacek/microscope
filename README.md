# Microscope

## What is Microscope?

Microscope is a set of tools that give you insights into how money influences politics.

- Discover top contributors of your government representatives as you browse their social media profiles.
- _More features coming soon!_

## Okay, now what

- Learn about the [_Citizens United v. FEC_ ruling and its impact](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Citizens_United_v._FEC) and [help reverse it](https://endcitizensunited.org/). 
- [Vote](https://www.vote.org/) for candidates who pledge not to accept donations from corporate PACs or federal lobbyists.

## See also

- [allaregreen.us](http://allaregreen.us/): expose the role money plays in Congress and highlight key election races
- [typpo/political-annotations](https://github.com/typpo/political-annotations): hover over the names of politicians in news articles you read to show contextual information, including contact info and a list of political contributors
- [Sunlight Foundation - Influence Explorer](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/sunlight-foundation-influ/fcmhooblihijmdgpojjoobibbnjbnicc): quickly view a company's political campaign contributions
- [Corporate PAC Sponsors](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/corporate-pac-sponsors/khbgkeagbjgndljefgnaogjcjjeappha): see which corporate PACs are the top sponsors of politicians
